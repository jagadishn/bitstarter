There are many sites that give financial information like stock quotes, news. Google Finance, Yahoo Finance and other financial web sites give lot of useful data that let you track your investments. They provide lot of tools like charting which lets you compare stocks and plot charts. They allow you to look at lot of data but they dont provide even simple answers such as how much your portfolio actually makes for you. Most portfolio tools including the ones provided by online brokerages are simple reporters of data. They do not analyze the data.

Here comes the new portfolio analyzer. This analyzer lets you know which of your stocks are winners or losers or simply keeping pace with overall markets. The analysis will compare your holdings with peers/competitors and market indices. The point is to provide knowledge by processing market information with your portfolio and provide insights that require time/effort which many of the casual or even professional individual investors do not have. The goal is to bring the power of market analysis using machine learning and data analysis techniques to your portfolio and provide actionable recommendations to you.

FAQ

Who are the potential customers?
Individual investors would be the first set of customers. Once there is a critical mass of technology the platform can be licensed to stock brokers such as ETrade.
What can it analyze?
It can analyze any portfolio consisting of US stocks, mutual funds and ETFs for which is there is trading information available.
